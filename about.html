About Us

We are half carbon, fully committed.

Our Carbon Connection
We are all made of carbon—nearly half of the human body’s dry mass is carbon. It’s the element of life, the foundation of everything we are and create. Yet the same element that sustains us has fueled industries and energy systems driving climate change to a tipping point.

Our Mission
At Fifty Percent Carbon, we stand at the crossroads of necessity and responsibility. Carbon is part of life, but humanity must cut its footprint by half to meet global climate goals. Our mission is to empower businesses, communities, and individuals to rethink, reduce, and offset their carbon impact.

Our Pledge
Fifty Percent Carbon is more than a name—it’s a call to action. Eliminating carbon entirely isn’t possible, but halving it opens new possibilities for a sustainable world. We are half carbon, fully committed, and ready to transform the way we live, work, and innovate.

Our Vision
To create a world where humanity thrives in harmony with the planet—cutting carbon impact in half while doubling the possibilities for innovation, sustainability, and a healthier future.

Why Fifty Percent Carbon?

  • Human & Environmental Connection: Half of our body is carbon, reminding us we’re part of the planet.
  • Climate Action: Aiming to cut carbon emissions by 50%, bold and aligned with global sustainability goals.
  • Balanced Awareness: A nod to our dependence on carbon while pushing for significant reduction.
  • Memorable & Thought-Provoking: Sparks curiosity, perfect for sustainability initiatives, green products, or lifestyle ventures.
